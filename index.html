Avner Kantor(detailed CV)

I am a doctoral student at the center for internet research at the University of Haifa.
My academic background is in philosophy and education while my work experience includes data science and software engineering. Currently, I am data scientist at the Corona national information and knowledge center.

My research uses data-driven methods to address questions of civic engagement and democratic processes. From 2014 to 2020 I developed the openpisa.org , an open data platform for the OECD's PISA research. In my current project I study audience engagement with data journalism by looking at comment section interaction.
